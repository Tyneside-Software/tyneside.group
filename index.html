Why this exists

Michael grew up in Seaham watching old industries go and too much reliance on subsidies. Career path: CloudTrade (£25.8m exit), Head of Engineering at Advanced, CTO & Co-Founder of Cloud Connect Software (independent company — main income that fuels this dream).

The goal is bigger than another exit: jobs and capability on Tyneside — for people entering work, for neurodivergent minds that need space to think differently, and for families who just need two hours of peace after a baby arrives.

Based at Howden Community Hub. First service area: Howden Ward. Then the next ward — as far as it keeps working.
