Why we’re doing this

Michael grew up in Seaham watching old industries disappear and too much reliance on government subsidies. He’s spent a career scaling software companies — CloudTrade exit at £25.8m, Advanced, now Cloud Connect at £600k ARR and growing fast — but the goal is bigger than another exit.

Build something that creates real jobs and real capability in Tyneside — not just for one family, but for the next generation.

Lewis starts part-time coding at 13. The daughters get the same opportunity. In seven years: seven years of professional experience. Generational wealth, generational capability, and a jobs engine for the region. Trickle-down economics done right — by building capability locally.
