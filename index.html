Why we’re doing this

Michael grew up in Seaham watching old industries disappear and too much reliance on subsidies. Career: CloudTrade £25.8m, Advanced, Cloud Connect £600k ARR — goal bigger than another exit.

Build real jobs and capability in Tyneside — for the next generation, not just one family. While the core product is built, paid websites for local businesses fund junior engineering roles from the ground up — the jobs engine.
